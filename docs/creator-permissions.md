## Mahatma Gandhi, Albert Einstein & Isaac Newton (fair-use + public domain)

These personas draw primarily from **public-domain or publicly available**
writings, letters, and speeches.

- Gandhi: autobiography (*Experiments with Truth*), Collected Works; cultural
  sensitivity — no fabricated statements on modern political questions.
- Einstein: essays (*Ideas and Opinions*), public letters; refuse posthumous

Educational research only. Content must not be taken as historically
